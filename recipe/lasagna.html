Lasagna


Description

A delicious Italian dish consisting of layers of cheeses, meat, and noodles. The key is layering these ingredients before cooking.


Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Monteray Jack Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Noodles
  2. Spread ricotta, marianara, and cheese on noodles
  3. Layer more noodles and repeat spreaing
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes
  5. Serve
