Pizza


Description

Dough needs to be made from flour, eggs, etc. Dough to be flattened into "pizza shape."

Sauce spread around the dough. Cheese goes on top of sauce. Toppings then are added and then everything is put into the stove for cooking.


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Make dough
  2. Flatten dough into pizza shape
  3. Spread sauce over top of dough
  4. Put cheese on top of sauce
  5. Place pepperoni's on top of cheese
  6. Place in oven and bake for 15-20 minutes
