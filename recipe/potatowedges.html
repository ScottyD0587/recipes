Potato Wedges


Description

Made from fresh cut potatoes, these wedges are a perfect pairing with any burger dish.


Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into wedges
  2. Put in baking pan and top with oil, salt, and pepper
  3. Bake for 20-25 min
